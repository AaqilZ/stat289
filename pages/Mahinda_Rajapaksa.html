Mahinda Rajapaksa

Percy Mahendra Rajapaksa ( Sinhalese : පර්සි මහේන්ද්‍ර රාජපක්ෂ ; born 18 November 1945 [1] ), more commonly known as Mahinda Rajapaksa ( Sinhalese : මහින්ද රාජපක්ෂ , Tamil : மஹிந்த ராஜபக்ஷ ; pronounced  [maˈhində ˈraːɟəˌpakʂə] ) is a Sri Lankan politician who was appointed as the Prime Minister of Sri Lanka on 26 October 2018 until a court issued an interim order preventing him from functioning in the position. [2] He previously served as the sixth President of Sri Lanka from 19 November 2005 to 9 January 2015.

Percy Mahendra Rajapaksa , MP ( Sinhalese : මහින්ද රාජපක්ෂ (පර්සි මහේන්ද්‍ර රාජපක්ෂ) , pronounced  [maˈhində ˈraːɟəˌpakʂə] , Tamil : மகிந்த ராசபக்ச ; born 18 November 1945 [1] ) is a Sri Lankan politician who served as the sixth President of Sri Lanka from 19 November 2005 to 9 January 2015.
