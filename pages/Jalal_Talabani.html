Jalal Talabani

Jalal Talabani ( Arabic : جلال طالباني ‎ Jalāl Ṭālabānī ; 1933 – 3 October 2017) [2] [3] was an Iraqi Kurdish politician who served as President of Iraq from 2006 to 2014, as well as the President of the Governing Council of Iraq . He was the first non-Arab president of Iraq , although Abdul Karim Qasim was of partial Kurdish heritage. [4] He is known as Mam Jalal (uncle Jalal) in the Middle East. [5] The surname Talabani means 'scholar' in native Kurdish.
